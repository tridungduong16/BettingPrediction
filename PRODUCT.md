# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Worldian serves football fans, analysts, and prediction-market users who want to understand an AI view of a World Cup match quickly. They arrive in a time-sensitive context, often before or during a match, and need to answer who is favored, how confident the model is, why the probability changed, and which markets deserve attention.

## Product Purpose

Worldian is an AI World Cup prediction market dashboard. It helps users understand what the AI currently believes about a match and why, combining match context, win probabilities, confidence, reasoning, market opportunities, probability movement, real-time AI updates, and chat. Success means a user can understand the likely winner, model confidence, key reasoning, recent changes, and interesting markets within five seconds.

## Brand Personality

Premium, analytical, and approachable. The interface should feel like a focused AI reasoning engine for sports predictions, not a sportsbook, trading terminal, or generic analytics dashboard.

## Anti-references

Avoid sportsbook clutter, Bloomberg-terminal density, traditional betting dashboards, complex quant interfaces, decorative SaaS marketing layouts, and AI-generated dashboard tropes such as generic identical card grids, weak hierarchy, and ornamental effects that do not support decision-making.

## Design Principles

1. Prediction first: the likely outcome and confidence must be immediately clear.
2. Reasoning before analytics: explain why the AI believes something before exposing secondary data.
3. Make change legible: probability movement and new information should show direction, magnitude, and cause.
4. Keep density purposeful: include rich market information without turning the dashboard into a trading terminal.
5. Support live use: status, updates, loading, and error states should make the system feel current and trustworthy.

## Market Coverage For Vietnam

Prioritize football markets that Vietnamese users commonly understand and play:

1. Kèo Châu Á (Handicap): examples include Brazil -1.0, France -0.5, Argentina +0.75.
2. Kèo Tài/Xỉu (Over/Under): examples include Over 2.5 goals and Under 3.5 goals.
3. 1X2 (kết quả trận đấu): home win, draw, away win, such as Brazil thắng, Hòa, France thắng.
4. Thẻ phạt: examples include Over 4.5 cards and whether there will be a red card.
5. Corner: examples include Over 9.5 corners and which team wins more corners.

Lower-priority prop markets can exist later, but these five families should define the default market cards and product examples.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA for contrast, keyboard navigation, focus visibility, and readable text. Do not rely on color alone for prediction direction or risk. Support reduced motion, responsive layouts, and clear states for loading, empty data, errors, and first-time use.
